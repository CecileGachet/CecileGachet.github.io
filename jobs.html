Topics of research

The Chair of Algebraic Geometry at the Faculty of Mathematics at Ruhr-University Bochum works in the field of complex algebraic and birational geometry.

Using methods from Mori theory and the study of families of rational curves on varieties, Fano and K-trivial varieties are investigated with regard to their symmetry groups, their possible fiber structures, their topology, the positivity of their tangent sheaf and other properties.

More modern notions such as Calabi—Yau pairs and singularities of the Minimal Model Program also play a role in this context.

You can also take a look at my recent publications here or email me if you want to know more about possible themes and topics for a PhD project under my supervision.
